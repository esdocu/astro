Community

Astro wouldn’t be what it is without its community. From Discord support, to documentation improvements, to translations, to code contributions, Astro welcomes it all.

Astro Team News

See several members of the Astro Team speaking at ViteConf 2024, a free online event for the web development community. Get your personalized ticket and show your Astro support!

These are some of the people who went above and beyond last month to make all our lives just a little (or a lot!) better:

Tips & Tools

The latest community-built utilities and integrations to help you build with Astro.

  • Astro Studio CMS - A dedicated CMS for Astro Studio built by the Astro community by @AdamMatthiesen, @Jumper, and @dreyfus
  • Advanced Astro Starter kit - Includes a pre-configured multi-language setup, and CodeStitch components by @buckybuck
  • Notion-based configurable static website generator - Your own Notion website with Astro by @nerdymomocat
  • Astro Infisical - An Astro integration for Infisical for secret management by @AdamMatthiesen
  • Bun adapter for Astro - Allows Astro to deploy your SSR site to Bun targets by @NeuroDev
  • Bag of Tricks - Inspection Chamber - See your view transitions like never before: examine all details, reveal, debug, and optimize! by @martrapp
  • Spaceship CMS - Coming soon, waiting list open by @blazingbowser
  • Star Warp - Add a quick “jump to first search result” URL to any Astro site using Pagefind by @Fryuni
  • @inox-tools/runtime-logger - Allows you to access Astro’s built-in logger from the runtime of a project by @Fryuni
  • astro-toolkit - A collection of Astro tools, components, and utilities to streamline your development process by @blackpepper
  • astro-typed-links - Automatically get typed links to your pages by @florian-levebre
  • astro-email-password-2fa - Built with Astro, SQLite, and Lucia v4 by @pilcrowOnPaper
