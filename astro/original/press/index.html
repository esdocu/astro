This is Astro

Astro is a web framework that takes the best from the early internet (think HTML and CSS) and puts it to work in a next-gen architecture to make every Astro site, and the whole web, faster. Whereas most frameworks today are designed for web apps, Astro is built for content-rich sites where performance matters most. Pull content from anywhere, deploy everywhere, and let's build the web we want, together.

Guidelines

How to use the Astro logo

  • Use our logo to link to Astro’s website or GitHub repository
  • Use our logo to show your product has built-in Astro support
  • Use our logo in a blog post or video about Astro

Things to avoid

  • Don’t use the Astro logo for your app or website’s icon
  • Don’t include the Astro logo in your own logo
  • Don’t modify the Astro logo or change its colors
  • Don’t sell products featuring the Astro logo without permission

Spacing

Always maintain adequate clear space between the Astro logo or logomark and other elements in your design.

Minimum size

The Astro logo and logomark should always be at least 24px tall in digital applications and 1/8” tall in print applications.
